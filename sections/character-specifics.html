The 3C(1) in air hit 6B > 5C > 3C(1) must be delayed on the following characters in order to prevent combos from dropping both when midscreen and in the corner: Hazama, Mu, Tager, Terumi, Tsubaki, and Valkenhayn.

Hakumen and Makoto require that 3C(1) is delayed when they're in the corner, though it is not required when they're midscreen.

Routes involving 5D > 5B > 5C > 236[A]~D will not work on Taokaka, Carl, Relius, Izayoi, Amane, Es, Bullet, and Platinum, due to their hurtbox being just far enough forward to where the activate whiffs. On long starters, this can be remedied by going straight into 5B > 236[A]~D, but be sure to confirm it quickly

Most routes involving 6B > 5C > 3C(1) > 22A will not work on Carl, Tsubaki, Hazama, Mu, Makoto, Valkenhein, Kokonoe, Terumi, Celica, Hibiki, Susan, and Mai, as they dont get popped far enough back for the lightning to hit.

Converting without setup after a TK (Tiger Knee) j.2C using j.2C > j.5D > j.A(1) > 236D isn't possible on Ragna, Makoto, Amane, and Es. Against Noel, Nu, Tsubaki, Hazama, Kagura, Terumi, and Lamda, it will only work in corner.
